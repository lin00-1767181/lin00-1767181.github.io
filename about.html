About Us

Who are we?

Lin's Company

Lin's Company specializes in all forms of group travel for leisure or business. Our women-owned travel agency strives to provide clients with the best value for their money and customizes each trip to exceed expectations. We have operated in the same Seattle location for the past 65 years. Suzy Smith has been the owner for over 35 years and her staff of specialists, including Mona Moreno, Cecilia Iverson, Joan Takasugi, Jackie Garmire, Donna Kehle, Josie Hazen, Connie Youel, Scott Smith, and Gina Busilacchi, represent over 100 combined years of travel and cruise service experience.

With expertise in any facet of travel arrangements, we save travelers and administrative personnel time while accomplishing desirable outcomes. Our team also responds quickly to support busy offices and individuals. Contact us for cruises, tours, vacation packages, and corporate travel. You will have an agent on call as needed.
